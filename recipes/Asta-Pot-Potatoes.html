Hage Village Pot Potatoes

Description

Asta's "Hage Tatoes" (pot potatoes) are the humble village staple from Black Clover that fuel the magic-less hero's endless energy. These crsipy tater tots made from grated potatoes capture the simple, satsfying peasant food that powers Asta's determination

Ingredients

  • 6 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes whole for 8-10 minues until partially cooked, then grate
  2. Squeeze out excess water using cheesecloth or towel
  3. Mix grated potatoes with flour and all seasonings.
  4. Form into small cylinders or balls (tater tot shape).
  5. Fry in 350°F oil until golden and crispy (3-4 minutes), drain on paper towels.
